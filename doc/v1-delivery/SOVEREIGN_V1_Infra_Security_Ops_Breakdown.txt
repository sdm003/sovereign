SOVEREIGN V1
Infrastructure, Security, Compliance, and Operations Breakdown
Version 1.1

1. Purpose

This document breaks V1 into non-feature platform work required to make the product deployable, supportable, and credible for first customers.


2. Scope

This stream covers:
- managed SaaS packaging and environment operations
- environment and secret management
- operational runbooks
- KYC and support boundaries
- monitoring and incident readiness
- backup and recovery operations
- release and rollback preparation


3. Infra / Security / Ops Epics

OPS-EPIC-001. SaaS deployment packaging and environment operations
OPS-EPIC-002. Environment, secrets, and key operations
OPS-EPIC-003. Monitoring, alerting, and incident response
OPS-EPIC-004. KYC and onboarding operations
OPS-EPIC-005. Support operating model and auditability
OPS-EPIC-006. Recovery, backup, and business continuity
OPS-EPIC-007. Production readiness and customer handoff
